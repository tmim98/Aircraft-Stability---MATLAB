Approximate digitization of Nelson 2nd ed. Fig. 2.29

Files:
- wingBody_kn_from_fig229.m
- figure_2_29_digitized_samples.csv

Method:
1) Treat the chart as a chained nomogram.
2) Digitize the straight-line families in pixel coordinates from the uploaded scan.
3) Interpolate between labeled families.
4) Convert the final y-coordinate to k_n.

Important:
- This is a first-pass digitization from a single scan shared in chat.
- It should be validated against 10-20 fresh hand readings from the book/PDF.
- The MATLAB function clips output to [0, 0.006] to avoid small scan-induced overshoots.
